Declaration of Independence of Contrapunctia

In the Year of Our Lord 2025, within the borders of a humble yet dignified estate, the People of this land — represented by their Founder — do solemnly proclaim the establishment of a free and sovereign micronation known henceforth as Contrapunctia.

Preamble

Recognizing the natural rights of all individuals to self-expression, creativity, and the pursuit of order through beautiful complexity, and affirming the sacred value of faith and knowledge, the People declare that:

  1. Contrapunctia is, from this day forward, an independent micronation, existing for cultural, artistic, intellectual, and spiritual enrichment.
  2. No external authority shall interfere with the internal symbols, traditions, institutions, or ceremonial governance of Contrapunctia, except in matters where applicable real-world laws must necessarily be observed.
  3. The capital city shall be Nova Europa, seat of culture and governance.
  4. The national currency shall be the Zlaty.
  5. Contrapunctia shall be guided by the principles of faith, reason, and justice, upholding the ideals of creativity, community, and divine order.
  6. All citizens, visitors, pets, and all other living beings are to be treated with dignity and respect under the laws of this land.

Declaration

With full respect for the realities of the wider world and an honest acknowledgement that international recognition is not the measure of a nation's worth, we pronounce Contrapunctia a sovereign nation.

Ratification

This Declaration is ratified by the Founder of Contrapunctia and will stand as the foundational proclamation upon which the laws, rituals, and institutions of Contrapunctia will be built.

Signed,

The Founder of Contrapunctia

Nova Europa, 2025
